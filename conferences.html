AI Conferences 2023

WAICF | World AI Cannes Festival 2023

Cannes, France | 9 February 2023 - 11 February 2023

The World AI Cannes Festival brings together those who are currently building the most game-changing AI strategies and use-cases on the planet, to an unparalleled experience in the vibrant future of AI! With hundreds of exceptional talks, showcases, performances, and networking happenings, this 2nd edition will be without any doubt the #1 marketplace dedicated to AI where you need to be seen.

Nvidia GTC

Online | 20 March 2023 - 23 March 2023

Nvidia GTC is a conference that brings together professionals from various fields, including artificial intelligence, computer graphics, data science, machine learning, and autonomous machines. The conference features talks and sessions from experts in these fields, as well as a keynote address from the CEO and founder of Nvidia, Jensen Huang.

MLConf | The Machine Learning Conference

New York City, USA | 30 March 2023

MLconf is a conference that brings together professionals and researchers interested in machine learning (ML) and its applications. It is a forum for sharing knowledge and ideas, networking with others in the ML community, and learning about the latest research and developments in the field.

World Data Summit

Amsterdam, The Netherlands | 17 May 2023 - 19 May 2023

The three days conference will help to get a better understanding of developing an analytical model for your business and customer growth. Experts will discuss all aspects of data analysis, how to work with unstructured data, how to upgrade data visualization and interpretability to the next level. Dig deeper into customer analytics or increase your technical knowledge by choosing a designated workshop day.

World Summit AI Americas

Montréal, Canada | 19 April 2023 - 20 April 2023

Now in its fifth year, World Summit AI Americas returns to Montréal and will connect the AI community in person over two packed days of AI content. This year is an opportunity to join the brightest AI brains and leading industry speakers and influencers from business, science and technology to discuss the hottest topics in AI. The full AI ecosystem will be joining us, including start-up’s, academics, investors, business leaders, and all the big tech companies.

CVPR | Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition

Vancouver, Canada | 18 June 2023 - 22 June 2023

The IEEE / CVF Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition Conference (CVPR) is the premier annual computer vision event comprising the main conference and several co-located workshops and short courses. With its high quality and low cost, it provides an exceptional value for students, academics and industry researchers.

AI & Big Data Expo

Vancouver, Canada | 30 November 2023 - 1 December 2023

AI & Big Data Expo is a showcase of next-generation technologies and strategies from the world of Artificial Intelligence & Big Data, an opportunity to explore and discover the practical and successful implementation of AI & Big Data in driving forward your business in 2023 and beyond.

NeurIPS 2023

Location to be defined | Dates to be published; probably in December 2023

The conference was founded in 1987 and is now a multi-track interdisciplinary annual meeting that includes invited talks, demonstrations, symposia, and oral and poster presentations of refereed papers. Along with the conference is a professional exposition focusing on machine learning in practice, a series of tutorials, and topical workshops that provide a less formal setting for the exchange of ideas.
